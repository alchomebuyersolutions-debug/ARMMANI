SO — The Sunday Open

The opening price when markets reopen after the weekend

The Sunday Open is the first price printed when the market reopens. It sets the tone for the entire week and acts as a key reference level for support/resistance.

Opening Times:
Forex: 17:00 EST • Futures/Gold/Indices: 18:00 EST
The SO is the first chance to react to weekend news, data releases, political events. Price often gravitates back to this level during the week.

PSO & EPSO — Previous Sunday Opens

Last week's and 2 weeks ago Sunday Open levels

These historical Sunday Open levels remain active as support/resistance. The PSO is especially powerful when the weekly gap is small — it acts as an additional layer of defense.

PSO = last week's open — another layer of S/R, especially when gap is small.
EPSO = two weeks ago — less reliable but still relevant at major confluences. Stack these with PoC/VaH/VaL for highest probability setups.

Session Breaks

Vertical lines marking the end/start of trading sessions

Session breaks help identify gaps and overnight moves. They visually separate Asia, London, and New York sessions so you know WHEN the real action happens.

Rule from Lorenzo: Trading setups have higher probability during core related sessions. For SPX/NQ = NYSE Open. For Gold = London + NY overlap. Session breaks help you stay in your lane.

Concept 4 of 6

Weekly Candle Formation

The weekly candle tells you who's winning the war

Weekly candles confirm broader trends. The Open = Sunday Open, Close = Friday close. The wicks tell you where the extremes were — and where the market rejected.

Top & bottom wicks are critical for execution. A long bottom wick = buyers stepped in hard (bullish). A long top wick = sellers rejected (bearish). Use weekly wicks to set SL/TP at meaningful levels.

FRVP — Fixed Range Volume Profile

Lorenzo's exact TradingView settings for Volume Profile

The Fixed Range Volume Profile is drawn manually over a specific price range to reveal where volume concentrated. Lorenzo uses specific settings for optimal detail.

Lorenzo's Settings: Row Size = 128 • Volume = Up/Down • Value Area = 70% • Extend Right = ON
Up/Down coloring shows you whether buyers or sellers dominated at each level. Blue = buyers, Yellow = sellers.
